Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip with Wanderlust Travel?

You can book a trip through our website, by calling our customer service, or by visiting one of our offices. Our travel advisors will guide you through the process and help you choose the perfect travel package.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), bank transfers, and PayPal. For certain packages, we also offer payment plans.

Can I customize my travel package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized travel experiences. Contact our team with your preferences, and we'll design a package tailored to your interests, budget, and schedule.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the trip and destination. Generally, cancellations made 60 days or more before departure receive a full refund minus a processing fee. Please refer to your specific booking terms for detailed information.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all our trips. We partner with reputable insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage options. Our advisors can help you choose the right plan for your journey.
